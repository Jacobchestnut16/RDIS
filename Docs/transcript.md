# HAITI Narriations
1. Haitian Declaration Pamphlet
“I carry the words printed in 1804. I declared that the people who overthrew French rule were now a sovereign nation.” (Spoken by: Maria Ciao)
2. Revolutionary Musket
“I was fired by soldiers who had once been enslaved. I helped them defeat the French forces that tried to restore the old order.” (Spoken by: Chase Flynn)
3. Cannon Fragment
“I belonged to captured French artillery. When the revolutionaries turned me against the empire that built me, the balance of power shifted.” (Spoken by: Chase Flynn)
4. Cutlass
“I was held in close combat by fighters who refused to return to bondage. Their persistence carried Haiti toward independence.” (Spoken by: Chase Flynn)
5. Early Haitian Flag
“I came from the French tricolor with the white removed. I signaled unity among people claiming freedom for themselves.” (Spoken by: Chase Flynn)
6. Louverture Letter Copy
“I contain the arguments Toussaint Louverture sent to France. I show the political struggle behind the military victories.” (Spoken by: Maria Ciao)
7. Dessalines Portrait
“My image marks the leader who commanded the final push to independence. His order in 1804 created the first Black republic.” (Spoken by: Adam Chestnut)

# USA NARRATIONS
1. Declaration of Independence
“I state the colonies’ break from Britain. In 1776 I justified independence through rights and consent of the governed.” (Spoken by: Brandy Chestnut)
2. Washington’s Sword
“I hung at the side of the Continental Army’s commander. I represent the military effort required to turn ideas into an independent nation.” (Spoken by: Chase Flynn)
3. First Newspaper Printing
“I carried the Declaration to readers across the colonies. I helped make rebellion a shared public cause rather than a local dispute.” (Spoken by: Maria Ciao)
4. Martha Washington’s Tea China
“I sat in a household shaped by political tension. Tea became a symbol of British taxation, and my presence reflects that conflict.” (Spoken by: Maria Ciao)
5. Continental Army Musket
“I was one of thousands used against British troops. My role shows how ordinary weapons sustained a long, uneven war.” (Spoken by: Chase Flynn)
6. Early American Flag
“I unified thirteen colonies under one emblem. I marked the decision to form a nation separate from the Crown.” (Spoken by: Chase Flynn)
7. Independence Proposal Draft - Maria Ciao
“I show the first written push for separation. I preserve the debates before independence was unanimously approved.”

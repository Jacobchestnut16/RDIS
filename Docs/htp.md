# How To Play

## Moving around

To move your characher around use your mouse and wasd.

## Listen to relics

When facing a relic press `e` to use your sonic to hear who or what the relic is.

## Going between times

US's independace day was in 1776 while Haiti's was in 1804, so you will have to time travel in order to visit the other's independance day.

Step into the Tardis (the blue box) and you can tavel between time and space.
